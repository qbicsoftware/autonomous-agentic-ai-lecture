Assume that the audience has a strong academic background in the field of computational biology and bioinformatics.

Assume that the audience is not very aware about cyber-attacks in generally and exfiltration attempts of
sensitive data.

Assume that the audience is familiar with chatbots, but not with how data is processed by third party
companies and that their data might be used for AI training. 

Assume that the audience is not familiar with abbreviations. Explain abbreviations on their first usage.

Assume that the audience are members of a core facility that process article 9 data according GDPR on
their local computers and have also customer communication following article 6 of GDPR. The core facility
processes data as part of a paid assignment of other researchers (service-oriented) and sometimes in a collaborative research
setting (research-oriented).
